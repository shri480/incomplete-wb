our products

CUTTER

Electrex cutters are the most popular and well-known tools in India. They are made with powerful motors for efficient and quick cutting. The cutter is compact, lightweight and double insulated to ensure no water damage to the motor. Electrex cutters are ideal for cutting tile, stone, granite, block, brick and marble.

DRILL

Electrex impact drills are the first choice for all types of civil, construction, electrical and other general work. Ergonomic and lightweight, Electrex drills are fitted with a powerful high torque motor, double insulation that makes it shockproof, and heat-treated gears for durability. The drills can be used to work on concrete, marble, granite, tiles, bricks, metal, wood, and other such materials

PLANER

The Electrex planer delivers power and cutting capacity with ease of use. Durable and built to last, it provides a smooth, even finish in the hardest of woods. The planer is designed in a manner, which directs the sawdust away from the operator hence further increasing ease of operation. The ergonomically designed handle provides greater comfort and less user fatigue.
